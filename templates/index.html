Al Bohling

Hello, I am Al. I am retired.

With the exception of military duty and formal education, I am a life-long resident of Louisa County in Iowa. I have a Bachelor of Science Degree and a Master of Landscape Architecture Degree with Minors in Art and Plant Ecology from Iowa State University. My career has been in the design and construction building industry. I have worked on major building projects throughout the United States. Pure function and aesthetics have always been at the forefront of my projects. In retirement I enjoy building on some of my earlier works and exploring new scenery.
